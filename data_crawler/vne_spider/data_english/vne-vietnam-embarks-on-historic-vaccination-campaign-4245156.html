The first shots of a Covid-19 vaccine produced by British-Swedish firm AstraZeneca have been administered in Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City (HCMC) and northern Hai Duong Province for frontline workers.At around 6:15 a.m., the first vaccine batches left Vietnam Vaccine JSC (VNVC) cold storage to be transported to the HCMC Hospital for Tropical Diseases.All workers in charge of AstraZeneca vaccination are trained beforehand.
