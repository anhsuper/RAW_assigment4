The photo collection is presented by YouTube account "Huong Vi Que Huong" (Flavor of Hometown), featuring channel owner Tho, a senior, gray-haired man with a warm smile.The channel is loved by many people for its aesthetic presentation of the countryside in the northern region. It has garnered over 750,000 YouTube subscribers and many videos have topped a million views.Tho's photographs present a nostalgic picture for many, helping them recall their childhood in rural areas during Tet, the most important festival of the year in Vietnam.This year, the Lunar New Year begins on February 12. The festival is a time when millions of families reunite, usually in their countryside homes.
